# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect contributors and maintainers to interact professionally and
respectfully.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discussing technical disagreements directly and constructively
- assuming good faith while still insisting on rigor
- keeping package-boundary debates factual and specific
- focusing review on correctness, safety, clarity, and maintainability

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- deliberate disruption of collaboration
- publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests,
and other contribution spaces tied to this project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or take other
reasonable moderation action when behavior violates this standard.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the
maintainers through the repository’s preferred private contact path.
